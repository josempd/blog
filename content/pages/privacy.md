## Analytics

This site uses [Umami](https://umami.is/), a self-hosted, open-source analytics tool. Umami does not use cookies, does not track users across sites, and does not collect personally identifiable information. All IP addresses are anonymised before storage. No data is shared with third parties.

## No Third-Party Trackers

There are no advertising networks, social media pixels, or external tracking scripts on this site. No data is sent to Google, Meta, or any other third party.

## Data Retention

Anonymised analytics events are retained for 12 months, after which they are deleted automatically.
